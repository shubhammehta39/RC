Our story

A quiet corner
above the city

The Ridge was born from a simple belief — that coffee should be worth pausing for. We source East African single-origin beans, roast them in small batches, and serve every cup with the attention it deserves.

Whether it's a working morning, a slow lunch, or an afternoon that refuses to end — you belong here.

Our craft

Every bean
has a story

From Kilimanjaro arabica to Rwanda red bourbon — we travel to find flavours that can't be replicated. Then we let the coffee speak for itself.

Our baristas train for months before they touch an espresso handle. That precision is what you taste in every cup.
